Overview

About the Workshop

Building on the previous Chicago-Tohoku Quantum Alliance workshops, this meeting will provide an in-person opportunity for members of the alliance to share recent progress, deepen research exchange, and discuss future directions for collaboration.

Current planning discussions include alliance-wide exchange across quantum materials, devices, information science, and related enabling technologies, together with possible sessions touching on quantum science and society, new facility introductions, and exhibition-related activities.

Audience

Participants

This workshop is intended for members and collaborators of the Chicago-Tohoku Quantum Alliance. Participation is expected to center on related researchers, faculty members, and invited contributors.

Attendance is expected to be limited to related members and invited participants.

Program

Program

  • Day 1: Senior-faculty talks, alliance updates, and invited research presentations
  • Day 2: Additional thematic sessions, poster exchange, and future collaboration discussions
  • Approximately 12 senior talks together with a poster session for younger researchers
