2nd Workshop on the Chicago-Tohoku Quantum Alliance

Overview

  • Date: May 15–16, 2025
  • Venue: David Rubenstein Forum, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, USA
  • Type: Workshop
  • Participants: 52 total (24 from Tohoku University and 28 from the University of Chicago and Argonne National Laboratory)
  • Format: Research presentations, collaboration discussions, and a student panel

Highlights

  • Opening remarks by Hideo Ohno and David D. Awschalom
  • Day 2 discussion on future joint research topics led by Ohno and Awschalom
  • Student panel discussion and networking session between the two universities
  • Follow-up themes included quantum computation, NanoTerasu collaboration, and student exchange
