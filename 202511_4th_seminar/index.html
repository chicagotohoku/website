4th Seminar of the Chicago-Tohoku Quantum Alliance

November 21, 2025 | Sendai, Japan

Overview

  • Date: Friday, November 21, 2025
  • Time: 14:15-15:45
  • Venue: Conference room, Laboratory for Nanoelectronics and Spintronics, RIEC, Katahira Campus
  • Type: 4th Seminar
  • Speaker: S. Carin Gavin
  • Affiliation: University of Chicago / Argonne National Laboratory / Tohoku University
  • Title: Optical Properties of Mixed-dimensional Heterostructures: Progress and Opportunities
  • Series note: Graduate Program in Spintronics Seminar / 149th Nano-Spin Engineering Seminar

Abstract Focus

  • Optical and electrical properties of mixed-dimensional heterostructures built from low-dimensional materials
  • Molecular control of 2D materials for quantum information science, including enhanced single-photon emission
  • Use of 2D materials as substrates to tune intermolecular optical interactions
